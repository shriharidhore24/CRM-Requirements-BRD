
# CRM Intelligent Lead Distribution & SLA Engine (Business Analysis Case Study)

## Project Overview
In enterprise B2B sales organizations, response velocity ("Speed-to-Lead") is the single greatest predictor of conversion rate. This project targets a classic revenue operations bottleneck: a manual lead-triage workflow that introduced an average 2- to 4-hour delay and resulted in **22% of inbound leads getting dropped entirely** within the first 24 hours.

As the Lead Business Analyst, I drove the requirement-gathering phase, stakeholder alignment workshops, and functional specifications to replace this legacy process with a fully automated, matrix-based routing engine combined with a high-priority, 15-minute SLA escalation protocol.

---

## Key Achievements & Business Impact
*   **Response Velocity:** Reduced manual lead assignment lag from 2–4 hours down to **under 3 seconds** automated delivery.
*   **Zero Dropped Pipelines:** Eliminated orphaned or unassigned leads via a deterministic fallback routing rule matrix.
*   **Accountability & Compliance:** Automated breach detection to ensure 100% visibility into neglected high-value accounts via real-time manager alerts.
